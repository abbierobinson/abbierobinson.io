Abbie Robinson

Research Profile

I am a PhD student at the University of Leicester specializing in the radiation environments of icy moons and planetary bodies. My work involves high-fidelity Monte Carlo simulations using Geant4 to model secondary particle cascades in planetary regolith. By characterizing these environments, I aim to quantify the habitability constraints and potential biosignatures on bodies like Europa and Enceladus.
